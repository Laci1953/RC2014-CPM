For TV100 terminals

The TREASURE game

You ('*') are in a mine full of very deep pits ('O').
It is told that in this mine, someone has wasted many precious coins ('$').
You have to collect all these coins.
Move from coin to coin, avoiding falling into one of the pits.

TREAS30.HEX is a customized version, for those of you who have Geoff's VT100 Terminal Kit ( https://www.tindie.com/products/petrohi/geoffs-vt100-terminal-kit/ ), to be played on a 30 lines x 80 columns screen.

Settings needed:

[Terminal] [Char set] = IBM PC 437
[Video] [Number of lines] = 30
